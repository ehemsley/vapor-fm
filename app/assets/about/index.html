vapor fm
Developer: Evan Hemsley
Location: Los Angeles, CA
Launched: Winter 2016
Description vapor fm began its existence as a simple auto-DJed internet radio station, which spun out of control until it became what it is now: an abominable hodgepodge of internet aesthetics and technology. vapor fm brings you a curated collection of music for a 24/7 vaporwave listening experience, celebrating the internet as a bold frontier of poor taste.
Technologies vapor fm is powered by:
  • three.js
  • Icecast
  • Liquidsoap
  • Brunch
Acknowledgments vapor fm would not be possible without the work of these excellent people:
  • Felix Turner, whose wonderful three.js tutorials made this endeavor possible
  • Mitch Gerrard, who created the bumps system and helped me review hundreds of albums
